jQuery Effects

fadeIn/fadeOut/fadeToggle(time in ms, callbackFunction) OR fadeIn() - makes the element transparent and hides it. Default duration: 400ms

slideUp/slideDown/slideToggle(time in ms, callbackfunction) OR just as slideUp() - modifies the height of the element and hides it.

.remove() method: removes the element after the effect duration is over and modifies the style accordingly.
